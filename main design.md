## 1. Introduction

Welcome to the VIOLET brand guidelines.

This document defines the core elements of the VIOLET identity system and explains how to use them consistently. Following these guidelines ensures a strong, recognizable, and meaningful brand voice.

---

## 2. Brand Overview

**Brand Name:** VIOLET  
**Tagline:** Family Beauty Lounge  

VIOLET represents elegance, softness, and premium beauty experiences.

---

## 3. Brand Mark

The brand identity consists of:

- Custom wordmark ("violet")
- Butterfly-inspired icon
- Combined logo system

---

## 4. Logo Design

### 4.1 Font Style

- Inspired by previous logo
- Minimal refinements applied
- Smooth, rounded, modern typography

### 4.2 Butterfly Icon

- Inspired by previous logo element
- Simplified and aligned
- Represents beauty, transformation, and elegance

## 5. Logo Variants

### Primary Logo
- Wordmark: "violet"

### Icon Logo
- Butterfly symbol + wordmark

### Display Logo
- Used for branding-heavy visuals
- Includes icon prominently

---

## 6. Logo Usage Guidelines

### ✅ Correct Usage

- Maintain original proportions
- Use approved colors only
- Keep clean and minimal

### ❌ Incorrect Usage

- Do NOT tilt the logo
- Do NOT apply gradients or patterns
- Do NOT add drop shadows or effects
- Do NOT outline the logo
- Do NOT distort or stretch

---

## 7. Background Usage

Approved background combinations:

- Purple background → White logo
- Light background → Purple logo
- Black background → White logo
- Soft pink background → Purple logo

Ensure strong contrast at all times.

---
